Computer Engineering

Introduction

Computer Engineering integrates electrical engineering and computer science to develop hardware and software. From designing microprocessors to building smart systems, it's the perfect blend of technical creativity — like Doraemon gadgets in real life!

Scope of Computer Engineering

  • Hardware Design and Development
  • Software Engineering
  • Embedded Systems
  • Networking and Telecommunications
  • Cybersecurity
  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Data Science and Big Data
  • IoT (Internet of Things)
  • Research and Development
  • Education and Training

Eligibility

Minimum Grade 8 completion with strong foundation in Math and Science. Passion for technology and curiosity to learn are key.

Official Recognition After Completion

After successfully completing the Diploma in Computer Engineering from a NEB/HSEB-affiliated institution like Janta Banbali Technical School, students are officially recognized as Computer Sub-Engineers or Overseers. This qualification is valid for applying to various positions in both governmental and private sectors of Nepal. Graduates are eligible to appear in Public Service Commission (PSC) exams, municipal and rural municipality jobs, and other technical service roles.
