OJT Experience: Computer Sub-Engineer at Naraha Rural Municipality

Overview

This On-the-Job Training (OJT) was completed at Naraha Rural Municipality as a Computer Sub-Engineer. The role focused on supporting municipal-level IT infrastructure and delivering digital services to rural wards, ensuring efficient governance and citizen service delivery.

Roles and Responsibilities

  • Provided technical support.
  • Installed and configured Windows OS and essential applications
  • Maintained and repaired computer hardware and peripherals
  • Set up local area networks (LAN) and configured internet access
  • Supported online fingerprint attendance and registration systems
  • Offered remote support using AnyDesk, TeamViewer, UltraViewer
  • Documented technical procedures and system configurations
  • Trained municipal staff in basic computer operation and security

Skills Gained

  • IT support and troubleshooting in real-world environments
  • Networking and system maintenance
  • Municipal-level system integration and deployment
  • Team communication and coordination
  • Technical documentation and reporting

Certification

Upon completion, the trainee received an official OJT certificate, verifying hands-on experience in technical support and municipal-level IT service delivery as a Computer Sub-Engineer.
